About Samantha

Samantha graduated in June 2024 from the University of Toronto with a Bachelor in Architecture and a minor in Urban Studies. She has a great interest in the intersection of art, sustainability and the built environment, she has taken on projects that try to solve problems with fun, imaginative solutions. She will be starting an internship at Disney Imagineering in Glendale, California in January 2025.
